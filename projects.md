# Portfolio Projects

## 1. Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard
- Analyzed monthly sales across Dublin, Cork, and Galway.
- Built KPI summary tables for revenue by region.
- Key insight: Dublin led total sales over the period.
- Files: retail_sales_data.csv, retail_sales_summary.csv.

## 2. HR Attrition Analysis
- Measured attrition patterns by department and age group.
- Calculated attrition rates and compared departments.
- Key insight: Sales showed the highest average attrition rate.
- Files: hr_attrition_data.csv, hr_attrition_summary.csv.

## 3. Student Performance Analysis
- Explored the relationship between study hours, attendance, and final scores.
- Created a scoring dataset for simple performance modeling.
- Key insight: Higher study hours and attendance improved final scores.
- Files: student_performance_data.csv, student_performance_sample.csv.
